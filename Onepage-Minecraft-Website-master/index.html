GAMEMODES

Stuff and things you can do on our server

HEARTS

Hearts are the key to your adventure on the Trust SMP. You begin your journey with 10 hearts. Every time you get an elimination, you earn an extra heart, making you even stronger. But be careful, because if you're slain, you lose a heart. Your heart count can grow until you reach 13 hearts, but the adventure doesn't stop there.

ECONOMY

On Trust SMP, our economy is focused around "Trade". You can trade by selling items like ores you've mined or items you've farmed. Once you have things to sell, you can use them to get what you need to play better.

POI

Points Of Interests (POIs) are the best way to get loot on the Trust SMP Network. Randomly scattered down the Outpost Roads are tons of different and unique POIs, which allow players to not only find loot for themselves but also find players to fight! Every POI has a chest that you can loot, and the server will remind everybody in chat when those chests will be refilled/refreshed.
